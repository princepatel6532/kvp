BUILDING TRUST
SINCE 2016

15+

KVP Allied Infra Pvt. Ltd. (formerly Klassic Waterproofing Inc.) is a fast-emerging contractor for Waterproofing, Flooring, Repair, Landscape and Paints, based in Delhi since October 2016.

Our promoters bring over 15 years of domain expertise, dedicated to delivering quality works at greater speed — resulting in complete peace of mind for the end user.

Built on mutual trust, honest business dealings and an unwavering commitment to world-class service delivery.

Our Vision

To be India's premier choice for specialty infrastructure works, setting benchmarks in quality, safety, and innovation while ensuring every structure we touch becomes a testament to durability and engineering excellence.

Quality Policy

We are committed to uncompromised quality through rigorous material testing, adherence to international standards, and continuous skill up-gradation of our workforce to deliver zero-defect results that exceed client expectations.

Our Team

Our strength lies in a multi-disciplinary team comprising highly qualified structural engineers, specialized site supervisors, certified applicators, and a responsive customer support wing, all working in synergy to deliver excellence.

Pan India

With a robust logistical framework and a mobile workforce, we have successfully executed landmark projects across 15+ states including Delhi NCR, Gujarat, Maharashtra, Karnataka, and the North Eastern regions, handling diverse climatic challenges.
